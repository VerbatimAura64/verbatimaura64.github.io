      4. The two assignments below go hand in hand. To briefly explain, the assignment was simple: come up with a question you want answered using the tools EXIF and BVI. The question that I came up with was whether or not adding a geotag to a picture on social media would make a difference in whether or not the GPS metadata got removed. Usually what happens on social media such as Facebook and Instagram is that the data gets removed without a second notice. However, what if the client agrees to tagging their location? Does that data still get removed? Does it get modified? To find out, click on the first link below to find out what the answer was. Click on the second link to read up on some answers about questions people might have about EXIF or BVI.
